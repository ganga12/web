About Us

In early 2015, Artrue Gallery relocated to an architectural gem situated across from Da’an Forest Park, the Central Park of Taipei. The building is graced with a landscaped entrance, high ceilings with fair faced concrete walls, unpolished concrete floors and two terraces that boast treetop views of the park, providing a profound and unique experience for appreciating artwork. Founded in 2011 in Taipei, Taiwan, Artrue International, the parent company of Artrue Gallery, was established to be a bridge and platform for diverse sources of social capital, creative capital and financial capital. With a global network of culture care stewards, Artrue holds exhibitions, events, and seminars, as well as assists our clients to build distinguished art collections.
